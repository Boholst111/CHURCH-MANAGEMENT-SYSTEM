## Managing Members

### Viewing Members

The Members page shows all church members in a table:
- **Photo** - Member's profile picture
- **Name** - Full name
- **Email** - Contact email
- **Phone** - Contact number
- **Membership Type** - Regular, Associate, or Visitor
- **Small Group** - Assigned small group
- **Status** - Active or Inactive

### Searching for Members

Use the **search bar** at the top:
1. Type a name, email, or phone number
2. Results update automatically as you type
3. Clear the search to see all members again

### Filtering Members

Use the filter dropdowns to narrow your view:
- **Status**: All, Active, Inactive, Archived
- **Membership Type**: All, Regular, Associate, Visitor
- **Small Group**: Filter by specific group

### Adding a New Member

1. Click the **"+ Add Member"** button
2. Fill in the member information:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Email** (required)
   - **Phone**
   - **Membership Type**
   - **Small Group**
   - **Photo** (optional)
3. Click **"Save"** to add the member

**Tip:** Required fields are marked with a red asterisk (*)

### Editing a Member

1. Find the member in the list
2. Click the **"Edit"** button in the Actions column
3. Update the information
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Archiving a Member

To archive a member (they won't appear in regular lists):
1. Find the member
2. Click **"Archive"** in the Actions column
3. Confirm the action

**Note:** Archived members can be restored later from the Archived filter.

### Viewing Member Details

Click on a member's name to see their full profile:
- Contact information
- Membership history
- Small group participation
- Event attendance
- Giving history (if you have permission)

## Finance Management

The Finance section has six tabs for different financial tasks.

### Overview Tab

Your financial dashboard showing:

**Summary Cards:**
- **Total Balance** - Current financial position
- **Monthly Offerings** - This month's offerings
- **Monthly Expenses** - This month's expenses
- **Budget Utilization** - Percentage of budget used

**Charts:**
- **Income vs Expenses** - 12-month comparison
- **Expense Breakdown** - Expenses by category

**Recent Transactions:**
- Last 10 financial transactions
- Quick view of recent activity

### Offerings Tab

#### Recording an Offering

1. Click **"+ Record Offering"**
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Date** (required)
   - **Offering Type** (Tithes, Special Offerings, Missions, Building Fund)
   - **Amount** (required)
   - **Payment Method** (Cash, Check, Bank Transfer, Online)
   - **Donor** (optional - leave blank for anonymous)
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Click **"Record"**

#### Viewing Offerings

The offerings table shows:
- Date of offering
- Type of offering
- Amount
- Payment method
- Donor (if not anonymous)
- Status (Recorded, Verified, Deposited)

#### Filtering Offerings

Use the filters to find specific offerings:
- **Date Range** - Select start and end dates
- **Offering Type** - Filter by type
- **Payment Method** - Filter by how it was paid
- **Status** - Filter by verification status

**Summary Metrics** show:
- Total offerings for the filtered period
- Number of offerings
- Average offering amount

### Expenses Tab

#### Adding an Expense

1. Click **"+ Add Expense"**
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Date** (required)
   - **Description** (required)
   - **Category** (Utilities, Salaries, Maintenance, Supplies, Ministry)
   - **Amount** (required)
   - **Fund** (General Fund, Building Fund, Missions Fund)
   - **Receipt** (upload receipt image/PDF)
   - **Notes** (optional)
3. Click **"Submit for Approval"**

#### Expense Approval Workflow

Expenses go through these stages:
1. **Pending** - Waiting for approval (yellow badge)
2. **Approved** - Approved but not yet paid (green badge)
3. **Paid** - Payment completed (blue badge)
4. **Rejected** - Not approved (red badge)

**If you have approval permission:**
- Click **"Approve"** to approve an expense
- Click **"Reject"** to reject an expense
- Add comments explaining your decision

#### Marking Expenses as Paid

After an approved expense is paid:
1. Find the expense in the list
2. Click **"Mark as Paid"**
3. Confirm the payment

### Budgets Tab

#### Viewing Budgets

Budget cards show for each category:
- **Category name**
- **Allocated amount** - Budget for the year
- **Spent amount** - Amount used so far
- **Remaining amount** - Budget left
- **Progress bar** with color coding:
  - 🟢 Green = On track (< 75% used)
  - 🟡 Yellow = At limit (75-100% used)
  - 🔴 Red = Over budget (> 100% used)

#### Creating a Budget

1. Click **"+ Create Budget"**
2. Select the **Fiscal Year**
3. Add budget line items:
   - **Category**
   - **Allocated Amount**
4. Click **"Add Another"** for more categories
5. Review the **Total Budget**
6. Click **"Save Budget"**

### Reports Tab

Generate financial reports:
- **Financial Summary** - Overall financial report
- **Offerings Report** - Detailed offering analysis
- **Expenses Report** - Expense breakdown
- **Budget Report** - Budget vs actual comparison

**To generate a report:**
1. Click on the report type
2. Select **Date Range** (or use presets like "This Month")
3. Choose **Format** (PDF, Excel, CSV)
4. Check **"Include Charts"** if desired
5. Click **"Generate Report"**
6. Report will download automatically

### Settings Tab

Configure finance settings:
- Default offering types
- Expense categories
- Fund names
- Fiscal year start date
- Approval workflow settings

## Activity Log

## User Management

Manage who can access the system and what they can do.

### Viewing Users

The Users page shows:
- User photo and name
- Email address
- Role (Admin, Pastor, Staff, Volunteer)
- Permissions summary
- Last login time
- Status (Active, Inactive, Pending)

### User Roles

**Administrator:**
- Full system access
- Can manage all users
- Can change all settings

**Pastor:**
- Access to all church data
- Can manage members and events
- Can view financial reports

**Staff:**
- Access to assigned areas
- Can manage members and events
- Limited financial access

**Volunteer:**
- View-only access
- Can see member directory
- Can view events

### Adding a User

1. Click **"+ Add User"**
2. Fill in user information:
   - **Name** (required)
   - **Email** (required)
   - **Role** (required)
   - **Password** (required for new users)
   - **Photo** (optional)
3. Click **"Create User"**
4. User receives a welcome email

### Inviting a User

For users who will set their own password:
1. Click **"Invite User"**
2. Enter their **email address**
3. Select their **role**
4. Click **"Send Invitation"**
5. They receive an email with setup link

### Managing Permissions

To customize what a user can do:
1. Find the user in the list
2. Click **"Permissions"**
3. Check/uncheck permissions by module:
   - **Members**: View, Add, Edit, Delete
   - **Finance**: View, Add, Edit, Delete, Approve
   - **Events**: View, Add, Edit, Delete
   - **Settings**: View, Edit
4. Click **"Save Permissions"**

**Tip:** Use "Toggle All" to quickly select/deselect all permissions for a module.

### Deactivating a User

To temporarily disable access:
1. Find the user
2. Click **"Deactivate"**
3. Confirm the action

**Note:** Deactivated users can be reactivated later.

### Deleting a User

To permanently remove a user:
1. Find the user
2. Click **"Delete"**
3. Confirm the action

**Warning:** This action cannot be undone. Consider deactivating instead.

## Accessibility Features

We've built the system to be accessible to everyone.

### For Keyboard Users

- **Tab Navigation**: Press Tab to move between elements
- **Enter/Space**: Activate buttons and links
- **Arrow Keys**: Navigate menus and lists
- **Escape**: Close dialogs and menus

- **Semantic HTML**: Proper structure for easy navigation
- **ARIA Labels**: Descriptive labels for all elements
- **Live Regions**: Announcements for dynamic content
- **Skip Links**: Jump to main content quickly
- **Heading Structure**: Logical heading hierarchy

**To skip to main content:**
- Press Tab when page loads

- **High Contrast**: All text meets WCAG AA standards
- **Zoom Support**: Works up to 200% zoom
- **Dark Mode**: Reduces eye strain in low light
- **Large Text**: Readable font sizes throughout
- **Clear Focus**: Visible focus indicators

- **Large Click Targets**: Minimum 44x44 pixels
- **No Time Limits**: Take your time with forms

- **Undo Options**: Recover from mistakes

### Adjusting Text Size

**In your browser:**
- **Zoom In**: Ctrl/Cmd + Plus (+)
- **Zoom Out**: Ctrl/Cmd + Minus (-)

### Using Dark Mode

1. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right
2. Click **"Theme"**

## Troubleshooting

### Page Not Loading

**If a page won't load:**
1. **Refresh the page**: Press F5 or Ctrl/Cmd + R
2. **Clear your cache**: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Delete
3. **Try a different browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
4. **Check your internet**: Make sure you're connected

### Can't Log In

**If you can't log in:**
1. **Check your email and password**: Make sure Caps Lock is off
2. **Reset your password**: Click "Forgot password?"
3. **Clear browser cookies**: May fix login issues
4. **Contact your administrator**: They can reset your account

### Data Not Showing

**If data isn't appearing:**
1. **Check your filters**: Clear all filters and try again
2. **Refresh the page**: Data may need to reload
3. **Check your permissions**: You may not have access
4. **Try a different page**: See if other pages work

### Form Won't Submit

**If a form won't submit:**
1. **Check for errors**: Look for red error messages
2. **Fill required fields**: Fields with * are required
3. **Check your internet**: Connection may have dropped
4. **Try again**: Click submit again after fixing errors

### Images Not Loading

**If images won't display:**
1. **Check your internet**: Slow connection may delay loading
2. **Refresh the page**: Images may load on refresh
3. **Try a different browser**: May be a browser issue
4. **Check file size**: Large images may take time

### Mobile Issues

**If having problems on mobile:**
1. **Update your browser**: Use the latest version
2. **Clear app cache**: In browser settings
3. **Try landscape mode**: Some features work better
4. **Use desktop site**: If mobile view has issues

### Performance Issues

**If the system is slow:**
1. **Close other tabs**: Free up browser memory
2. **Clear browser cache**: Remove old data
3. **Check your internet**: Run a speed test
4. **Restart your browser**: Fresh start may help

### Getting Help

**If you still need help:**
1. **Check this guide**: Search for your issue
2. **Ask your administrator**: They may know the answer
3. **Contact support**: Provide details about the issue:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Any error messages
   - Your browser and device

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Will my data be lost with the new interface?**

A: No! The new interface is just a visual update. All your data remains exactly the same.

**Q: Do I need to learn everything new?**

A: No. Most features work the same way, just with a better look. This guide will help you with any changes.

**Q: Can I switch back to the old interface?**

A: During the transition period, your administrator may allow switching between old and new. Eventually, everyone will use the new interface.

**Q: Will this work on my phone?**

A: Yes! The new interface works great on phones, tablets, and computers.

### Using the System

**Q: How do I change my password?**

A: Click your profile picture > Settings > Security > Change Password.

**Q: How do I update my profile picture?**

A: Click your profile picture > Profile > Upload Photo.

**Q: Can I customize the colors?**

A: Administrators can customize the church's branding colors in Settings > Church Information.

**Q: How do I enable dark mode?**

A: Click your profile picture > Theme > Select "Dark" or "Auto".

### Data and Privacy

**Q: Who can see my information?**

A: Only users with appropriate permissions can see your data. Administrators control access levels.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**

A: Yes. Administrators can configure automatic backups in Settings > Backup & Restore.

**Q: Can I export my data?**

A: Yes. Most pages have an "Export" button to download data in various formats.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**

A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**

A: No. It works in your web browser, no installation needed.

**Q: What if I find a bug?**

A: Report it to your administrator with details about what happened.
